Electric Vehicle Charging Stations

Welcome to Our Project

Welcome to our innovative project at the intersection of machine learning and sustainable technology! Our endeavor focuses on harnessing the power of predictive modeling to determine optimal locations for electric vehicle (EV) charging stations. Using sophisticated machine learning algorithms, we analyze diverse census data predictors to identify and map out strategic areas for these stations. Our goal is to enhance accessibility and convenience for EV users while contributing to a greener future. Join us on this exciting journey where technology meets sustainability!
